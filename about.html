About Me

BIO

Hi! I'm a junior at Brown University studying Computer Science. Outside of CS, I love learning languages. From French to Italian to ancient Egyptian Hieroglyphs, language is my favorite way to learn about the world and explore different cultures. As I strive to find my path, I hope to combine my passions in CS, applied maths and languages.

In my free time, I love playing the ukulele, singing and acting.
